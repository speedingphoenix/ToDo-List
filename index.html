How this to-do list works!

This is a todo list that you make yourself. The list does not have storage, meaning when you close the site or refresh, the list will be gone. Just a heads up!

If you click on the things you added, than a check symbol will appear and a line will cross the thing. To remove something from the list, click on the lines right top corner.
